About Us 

We are a company who believes in letting your mind be creative and work with a positive attitude. That’s why we also offer a place to let your mind thrive and be creative so we can fulfill that belief along with you going along the journey with us as well. We think that everybody deserves a chance in society to be creative and inventive.

We strive to let people be creative and make any inventions you have in mind. We believe that everyone has lots of potential to be an aspiring and successful change that the world needs. Our main customers are people who like to use their brains and really think hard about things they want to do. They like to be curious, ask a lot of questions, use their imagination for the better of the world, learn from their mistakes, and follow their intuition for the betterment of the world.

Pricing & Plans

You can sign up at your local makerspace. No digital signups for any plans are offered.   

Starter

Our “Starter” plan is nice if you are just trying to see if our makerspace is the kind of place that would help you. This pricing plan is also good if you will mostly just be doing simple projects. What also makes this a good deal if you just want to use the makerspace occasionally, in this plan you get access to basic materials and supplies.

$9.99/mo

Intermediate

With the “Intermediate” plan you have access to lots of tools and materials and access to some of our most expensive utilities. This plan is also good if you are building basic things. This is a good plan if you are more into engineering and maybe models and things like that.

.

 

$49.99/mo

Professional

With the “Professional” plan, you will get full access to our makerspace, and you will get access to a more private area to do your work in. This plan is also good for doing very complex projects and making advanced digital art or anything of the sort.

 

 

$99.99/mo
